Old Drawings

These are some old drawings I did while living in Oakland, California, all from the years of 2007 to 2017.

A lot of them are studies of old photos and printed matter I found at various thrift stores in the East Bay, while others are drawings of people I'd see in my neighborhood around the MacArthur BART station between Telegraph Avenue and MacArthur Boulevard.

A few are of my mom and dad.
