--- layout: page title: Autodesk Email Wireframe permalink: /autodesk-email-wireframe/ ---

This project was conceived as a way to help Autodesk marketers unify and simplify their email campaigns and nurture programs. By using a starter, or “wireframe” template, email messages being deployed from Marketo could stay consistent with and match Autodesk brand guidelines. In addition to this, there could be a quicker turnaround for campaigns because less time was spent coding new email templates from scratch.

Details

  • Project Start Date: September 2015
  • Project Completion Date: November 2015
  • Marketing Automation Platform: Marketo
  • Languages Used: HTML and CSS for the email wireframe; HTML, Sass and jQuery for the Email Style Guide.
